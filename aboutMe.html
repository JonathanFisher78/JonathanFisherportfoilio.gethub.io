My Story

I grew up on a farm in Moreland Georgia where I learned the important of hard work initiative. I discovered my love for creating and problem solving at a young age. During my childhood I made a lot of projects like building doors for the chickens to get through that predators can’t, building a barn, or fixing cars with my dad. I worked a lot with more mechanical engineering type fields until middle school exposed me to the world of computers and robotics. I attended a summer camp for Lego EV3 robotics and was hooked instantly. The idea of building and programming something to complete task autonomy was amazing to me. In high school I taught myself python, Arduino, and some basic electrical wiring. I coded an ad blocker for my house, robotic arm, and even a small prototype vending machine for my school. Now I am in collage and am looking for new opportunity’s to learn and further develop my skills through clubs and organizations.
